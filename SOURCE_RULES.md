# Source Rules - V2

## Preferred source order

1. eBay Product Research / Terapeak
2. eBay sold/completed listings
3. WorthPoint for vintage, rare, antique, and collectibles
4. Platform-specific sold comps such as Mercari, Poshmark, Grailed, Depop, Etsy, StockX, GOAT, Reverb, and Discogs
5. Google Search for identification and rare-version research

## Rules

- Prefer sold prices over active asking prices.
- Do not average active prices as sold comps.
- Record source URLs in `MARKET_RESEARCH`.
- Separate normal comparable comps from rare/high-ceiling examples.
- If fewer than 5 reliable comps are found, mark `PARTIAL_RESEARCH`.
- If a source is useful only for identification, say so.
- Use confidence labels: HIGH, MEDIUM, LOW.

## Research output

Every item should attempt to capture:

- low sold price and description
- average sold price and comp count
- max sold price and explanation
- rare/original/sealed/limited notes
- recommended list price
- source URLs
